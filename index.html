Commonality

We live in a world that’s more connected than ever — but somehow, people are feeling more disconnected than ever. Real-life friendships are harder to come by, and it’s not uncommon to feel like no one around you shares your interests. With the slow disappearance of “third spaces” like coffee shops, clubs, and community centers, plus the rise of screen-heavy, isolated lifestyles, it’s easy to lose touch — not just with people, but with the hobbies and passions that make us feel like ourselves.

That’s where our app comes in. We believe no one should have to give up what they love just because they don’t know someone else who loves it too. Our platform makes it easy to find people who get it — whether it’s rock climbing, board games, improv, anime, or literally anything else. Just set up a profile, share your interests, and we’ll connect you with someone who wants to do that thing with you — in real life. Simple, human, and built for genuine connection.

Technologies Used:

HTML, Tailwind CSS, JS, TS, React, React Router 7, Express, PostgreSQL, Docker, Insomnia, Zod, Git/GitHub, Mailgun, REST APIs, Miro, DBDiagram

Etern.life Memorial Map

I created Etern.life when I was the Marketing Manager at Passages International, Inc. We had hired a few third-party developers who did not manage to bring this project to life, and I convinced my boss to give me some time to try to build it myself. For this project, I created a WordPress website and used a few plugins - mainly a business directory plugin - which I hacked up to become an online memorial website.

This website lets users mark the locaton that they scattered a loved one's ashes and create an online memorial. The location is pinned on a map, and anyone can see the memorial and where this person was scattered.

This website synced into an Oracle NetSuite database via custom API to verify purchases.

Etern.life is currently live, and has hundreds of memorials across 6 continents and many countries

Technologies Used:

WordPress, HTML, CSS, PHP, and a variety of customized WordPress plugins.

Turning Pointe Health & Wellness

Turning Pointe Health & Wellness was a medical clinic offering a combination of Eastern and Western medicine. After being hired on to create the website for this startup, I quickly learned that the company was missing a brand identity, a complete grasp of its offerings, and messaging. A major part of this project became a marketing/branding consulting project to decide what would go on the website.

This website was built using WordPress. It's a relatively simple design, but it highlights the branding and content creation work that I did for the company.

Unfortunately, due to a falling out between the founders, this company did not make it, but I'm working on putting a version of the website up here as part of my portfolio.

Technologies Used:

WordPress, HTML, CSS, PHP, and a variety of WordPress plugins.
